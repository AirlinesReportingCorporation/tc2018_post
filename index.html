ARC Awards

Each year, ARC honors an airline and travel agency for exceptional work that drives our industry forward. We are proud to announce our winners for 2018.

Airline Representatives of the Year

United Airlines

Revenue Accounting & Commercial Receivables Teams

The recipients of 2017’s Airline Representatives of the Year are the Revenue Accounting and Commercial Receivables departments at United Airlines. These two dedicated teams worked together to implement a chargeback management process that provides agents with an opportunity to respond to chargebacks and avoid the financial loss associated with chargebacks that can be won.
