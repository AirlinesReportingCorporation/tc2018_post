Airline Representatives of the Year

United Airlines

Revenue Accounting & Commercial Receivables Teams

The recipients of 2017’s Airline Representatives of the Year are the Revenue Accounting and Commercial Receivables departments at United Airlines. These two dedicated teams worked together to implement a chargeback management process that provides agents with an opportunity to respond to chargebacks and avoid the financial loss associated with chargebacks that can be won. This initiative has been recognized as an industry best practice for managing agency chargebacks and is leading the way in partnering with the agency community to constantly evaluate and improve an often frustrating process that has a direct impact to the bottom line.
